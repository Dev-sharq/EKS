Steps to install Velero Client on Ubuntu:

#Install Velero binary:

wget https://github.com/velero-io/velero/releases/download/v1.18.2/velero-v1.18.2-linux-amd64.tar.gz

#####Extract the tarball:

tar -xvf velero-v1.18.1-linux-amd64.tar.gz -C /tmp

#######Move the extracted velero binary to /usr/local/bin

sudo mv velero-v1.18.2-linux-amd64/velero /usr/local/bin
